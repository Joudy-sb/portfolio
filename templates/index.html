Achievements 🎉

Hackathon Winner

My team and I won first place in the #Coding4Integrity Hackathon, sponsored by the United Nations Convention Against Corruption (UNCAC), ACTA of Qatar, and Microsoft, competing against 23 teams from 17 Arab countries. Our project focused on enhancing transparency in public procurement to combat corruption, particularly inspired by Lebanon's challenges. We leveraged Microsoft Azure services to implement a solution that prioritized transparency, integrity, and impartiality.

Beyond an Engineer: Best Idea Award

My team and I won the "Best Startup Award" at the Beyond an Engineer startup competition for our mobile app, HERITAGE Hunt, which promotes Lebanese heritage and culture. The app engages users through weekly challenges and rewards, such as discounts at local businesses, while encouraging city exploration and supporting small entrepreneurs. Organized by AIESEC, IEEE - AUB, and DDD AUB club, the competition recognized our innovative approach to combining cultural engagement with local business support.
